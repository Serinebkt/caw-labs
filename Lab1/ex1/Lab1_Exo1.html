Brief History

The history of Palestine dates back thousands of years, serving as a crossroads of civilizations between Africa and Asia.

Throughout history, it has been ruled by various empires including the Romans, Ottomans, and the British, each leaving a deep cultural impact on the region.

In the modern era, the Palestinian people continue their struggle for independence and recognition as a sovereign state.
